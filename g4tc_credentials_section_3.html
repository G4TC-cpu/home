About the advisor

The numbers tell a story.
I make sure yours tells the right one.

I am a Maryland-based financial advisor and community reinvestment strategist. I hold the NACAT Pros Certified Cannabis Seed Counter designation — the only nationally recognized cannabis accounting and tax credential — along with NACVA membership, NATP professional standing, and MCBA Ally status.

My practice has two distinct lanes. On the cannabis side, I guide social equity licensees through the financial and compliance infrastructure they need to get funded, stay compliant, and open on time. On the community side, under G4TC C.A.R.E.S., I position neighborhood organizations and community founders to understand and access Maryland's CRRF funds before those windows close.

I do not just know the rules. I understand what it feels like to be on the side where the rules were never explained clearly. That is why I built this practice around making complex financial and regulatory systems accessible to the people they were designed to serve.
